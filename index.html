Privacy Policy

1. Information Collection

We collect basic personal information such as Name, Email, and Phone Number during the internship registration and contact form submissions to facilitate program operations and credential issuance.

2. Use of Information

The collected information is solely used for evaluation, communication during the internship, and generating verifiable certificates upon program completion.

3. Information Sharing

DevRise does not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties. We value your digital privacy and integrity.

4. Data Security

We implement secure methods to process registrations and store student records. Certificates public validation is restricted only to verifying credentials using unique IDs.

Terms & Conditions

1. Program Rules

Interns are expected to construct and code their own projects. Plagiarism or code copying from uncredited repositories without customization violates our code of conduct and leads to rejection.

2. Certificate Requirements

To receive the certificate, you must submit a working, code-complete project and documentation within the assigned program deadline (4 or 8 weeks). Verification details will be permanently searchable on our platform.

3. No-Refund Policy

Since the program is complimentary and unpaid, there are no refund policies or charges applied. Any external websites claiming fees on behalf of DevRise are fraudulent.

4. Re-attempt Policy

If you fail to submit your project on time, you can re-apply in the next cohort. We support and encourage continuous learning.
